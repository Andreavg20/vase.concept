Vase. Concept

V A S E. is a brand that was born with the intention of incorporating minimalism, versatility, and comfort into your daily life.
“Seasonless and endurable designs…”
We focus on developing garments with finishes that give a neat and delicate look both on the inside and outside. A characteristic that creates timeless piece with a strong voice. Our collections are consciously designed and produced in Atlanta following ethical and sustainable practices. Capsule collections and pre-order production helps us reduce waste.
“…made with natural fabrics”.

Linen

Linen is a natural fabric made from flax fibers. It is light, strong, durable, breathable and thermo adjustable which means it is cool during the Summer and warn during the Winter. linen garments also soften with wear.

Care guide:

  1. Linen garments can be both hand and machine washed.
  2. Cold hand wash or cold machine wash on a gentle cycle with similar fabrics and colors.
  3. Line dry in a cool and dark place.
  4. Steam out before wear.
  5. When storing garment, hang is preferred.

Cotton

Cotton is a natural textile made from fibers surrounding the seeds of cotton plants. It is a soft, fluffy, breathable and lightweight material. Cotton is likely to shrink so please follow the care guide instructions carefully.

Care guide:

  1. Cold wash with similar fabrics and colors.
  2. Line dry in a cool, dark place and steam out before wear.
  3. Avoid any bleaches
  4. Depending on garment type you can either store it flat (knitwear such as sweatshirts or t-shirts) or hang it on the rack (woven garments such as shirts or trousers).
